abstract: In March of 2021, the Santa Fe Institute hosted a workshop as part of
  its Foundations of Intelligence in Natural and Artificial Systems project.
  This project seeks to advance the field of artificial intelligence by
  promoting interdisciplinary research on the nature of intelligence. During the
  workshop, speakers from diverse disciplines gathered to develop a taxonomy of
  intelligence, articulating their own understanding of intelligence and how
  their research has furthered that understanding. In this report, we summarize
  the insights offered by each speaker and identify the themes that emerged
  during the talks and subsequent discussions.
