# Differentiable Neural Computers
<p style= "text-align: right"><i>Created 26/10/2021</i></p>

## What can they do?
- perform classic computations such as graph traversal[^paaßen]

## Where do they fail?
- building rich enough representation to reach strong relational reasoning skills[^park]
- in general, application to real world tasks seem still out of reach


## What are the hard parts?
- require large datasets and long training time
