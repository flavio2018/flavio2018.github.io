# What do ANN models model?

There is a quantity of literature on this subject, especially in the field of Cognitive Psychology.  
  
A good insight is incidentally given by the work of Chaabouni et al. [[@chaabouni_communicating_2021]]), in which it is pointed out that ANN are not necessarily biologically plausible ([[€5_realismo_modelli_computazionali]]). However, they show emergent properties which can be similar to those of individual humans or groups of them.
  
In these cases, conditions which caused the emergence of this similar behaviour can be studied as possible causes of that behaviour in humans, too.
