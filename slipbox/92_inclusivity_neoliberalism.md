# Inclusivity, neoliberalism & liberty
Neoliberalism and capitalism work *with the minorities*, not against them. One result for this strategy is to be able to have more workforce. Perhaps the less intuitive outcome is linked to individualization: self-realization of a person *as an individual* (i.e. becoming whoever you want to be) actually favors the individualistic and competitive model of society that allows neoliberalism and capitalism to flourish[^harari].
