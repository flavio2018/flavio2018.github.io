According to Núñez, there are at least four cultural preconditions for the emergence of arithmetical abilities in human populations.  
  
Indeed, linguistic abilities and familiarity with symbolic manipulation are not sufficient cultural preconditions for the developement of arithmetics.  
  
The four elements identified by Núñez are:  
- language  
- symbolic manipulation  
- imagination & metaphore  
- culture-based preoccupations and practices.  
  
It is conceivable that a model of human arithmetical abilities should include one or more of these elements.
