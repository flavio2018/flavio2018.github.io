# Platform business model
[Platform Design Toolkit](https://platformdesigntoolkit.com/)

According to Deloitte[^platform],
> What we mean with a platform business is a business model (not a technology infrastructure) that focuses on helping to facilitate interactions across a large number of participants. 
> [...] 
> The role of the platform business is to provide a governance structure and a set of standards and protocols that facilitate interactions at scale so that network effects can be unleashed.

So, what I was trying to create with Syn was a learning platform (one of the main kinds of platforms), and SOS can also be seen as a learning platform.

[^platform]: https://www2.deloitte.com/ch/en/pages/innovation/articles/platform-business-model-explained.html
