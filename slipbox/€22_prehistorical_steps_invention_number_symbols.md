The work by d'Errico et al. ([[@derrico_number_2018]]) proposes a series of phases which could have actually happened and brought humans to use number symbols (as well as other symbolical and ornamental artifacts).  
  
The proposed phases are:  
- casual creation of signs (e.g. notches) by manipulation of the substrate  
- voluntary reproduction of the signs on a substrate (why?)  
- identification of a meaning for each individual sign  
- addition of signs on the substrate after some time  
- assigning meaning to features of a sign.
