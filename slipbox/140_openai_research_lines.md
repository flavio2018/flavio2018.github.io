Looking at [their blog](https://openai.com/blog/tags/research/), it looks to me that OpenAI shifted from investigating on the emergence of language[^mordatch] to other kinds of problem such as language models with GPT, robot motor control, systematizing RL with Gym, and more.

Why did this happen? Was it a business need linked to the wider applicability of these technologies, or a theoretical insight motivating the focus on different topics rather than pursuing the investigation about the emergence of language?
