# Why writing?
<p style= "text-align: right"><i>Created 21/10/2021</i></p>

This can be considered as the second follow up of [[63_meta_my_knowledge_cycle]] about writing. I had started answering this question there, providing a perspective that is more related to studying.

One point, however, was already dedicated to writing for others (so that others can understand something, in the general case of writing essays).

I want to elaborate more on this point here. The deeper question is: why writing for others? What can be the drivers of this activity?

Surely, if done with systematicity and quality, it can result in economical gain. It can lead to make new experiences, such as meeting new people or going to more or less interesting venues. It can lead, in general, to build a reputation in small or smallish circles.

It can benefit the community you are writing to explain to[^distill] (which is also the philosophy behind Il Post).

This kind of writing can be done in journalistic form, in literary form (i.e. writing essays) or in a more tech-blog like form, such as writing blog posts about some technical implementation.

***

One alternative to writing is teaching, i.e. building a relationship with someone and pass knowledge and values to him/her by non-mediate interactions.

This kind of activity reaches a smaller number of people and has more of an emotional value for both the people involved than writing and learning one one's own.
