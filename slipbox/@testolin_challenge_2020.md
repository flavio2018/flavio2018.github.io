Challenge of modeling acquisition of mathematical concepts
---

-   _p.3_ There is evidence of item-by-item enumeration skills in several animal species, but lacking of full understanding of cardinality of a set seems to coincide with the lacking of number words.
    
-   _p.4_ According to Testolin, the key elements of more realistic models of mathematical learning should be: perceptual system, embodiment (with particular attention on hands), material representations, diversified learning signals (reinforcement learning), linguistic input.
    
-   _p.5_ One advantage of reinforcement learning in combination with manipulation of material representation, is the possibility to model the “imagination” of manipulation of artifacts, which would simulate internalized knowledge.
