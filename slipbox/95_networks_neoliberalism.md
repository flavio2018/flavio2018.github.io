# Networks and neoliberalism

Two articles[^arthur][^pasquinelli] in very different fields point out to a relatedness between neoliberal theorists like Friedrich von Hayek and scientific approaches that have gained attention in the scientific community in the latest 30 years and, interestingly, are both founded on the concept of networks.

Digging further on this line of research could lead to
1) shed light on the theoretical foundations of connectionist models of cognition[^ottimizzazione],
2) to have further insights regarding the points of connection between complexity science and connectionist models, as well as
3) to unveil the ideological foundations of both disciplines.
