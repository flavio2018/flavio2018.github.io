title: If It's Neutral, It's Not Technology
aliases: ["If It's Neutral, It's Not Technology"]
authors: Lance Strate
year: 2012
abstract: Taking a media ecology perspective, this article argues that technology cannot be neutral, because it is a form of change, and it has an inherent bias based on the properties of its materials and methods. Additionally, the application of a technology is an intrinsic part of the technology itself, as is technique, instructions, software, or knowhow. Moreover, the bias of technology cannot be reduced to the designer's intent, as innovations always have unanticipated, unwanted, undesirable effects.
