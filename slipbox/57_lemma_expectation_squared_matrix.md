# Lemma
*The expectation of a $p \times p$ matrix multiplied for another matrix from both sides*.

Given 
- a squared matrix $A$ of order $p$
- and the matrix $X$ of observations of a random vector, 
	- we have that $\mathbb{E}[X^TAX] = \mu^TA\mu + \textrm{tr}(AV)$, 
- where $\mu:=\mathbb{E}[X]$ 
- and $V:=\textrm{var}(X)$.
